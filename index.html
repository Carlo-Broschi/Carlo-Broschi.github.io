Research

I
Molecular phylogenetics

Evolutionary fate of the Sm/Lsm/Hfq fold across the three domains of life. Structure-informed phylogeny, genome reduction, DPANN/Asgard archaea.

bio-a · bio-b · bio-c
 II
Linguistic typology

Quantitative reanalysis of cross-linguistic databases (Grambank, WALS, AUTOTYP). Bayesian contact modeling, measurement invariance, IRT/DIF.

lang-a1 · lang-a2 · lang-a3
 III
Grammatical formalization

Typed feature structures and unification applied to subject selection, modality, switch-reference, and conditional typology. ALE · Grammar Matrix · ACE · RRG.

lang-b1 · lang-b2 · lang-c · lang-d · lang-e
 IV
Historical musicology

The transition from the castrato to the countertenor in Western art music. A two-axis argument distinguishing vocal mechanism from repertoire continuity.

music-a · music-b
 V
Philosophy of science

Comparative methodology and the problem of reference in biological and linguistic systematics. Developed in dialogue with the philosophy of special sciences.

phd-a · phd-b · phil-a · phil-b · phil-c · stat-a
